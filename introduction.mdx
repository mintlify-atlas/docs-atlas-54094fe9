## Welcome to DecipherIt

DecipherIt is a cutting-edge **AI-powered research assistant** inspired by Google NotebookLM that revolutionizes how researchers, students, and professionals explore, analyze, and synthesize information from the web.

Transform any combination of documents, URLs, or topics into comprehensive research notebooks complete with AI-generated summaries, interactive Q&A capabilities, audio overviews, visual mindmaps, and automatically generated FAQs.

## The Problem We Solve

Traditional research is time-consuming and fragmented. Researchers often struggle with:

  <Card title="Information Overload" icon="magnifying-glass">

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Input Your Research Sources">
    Enter any topic, upload documents, add custom URLs, or input manual text. DecipherIt accepts multiple formats simultaneously for comprehensive research.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Planning & Discovery">
    The Web Scraping Planner creates a strategic research plan. The Link Collector Agent uses Bright Data's search engine to find relevant sources globally, bypassing geo-restrictions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Intelligent Scraping">
    The Web Scraper Agent uses Bright Data's `scrape_as_markdown` tool to extract content and convert it to clean, structured markdown format automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Analysis & Synthesis">
    Research Analyst agents synthesize information from multiple sources while Content Writer agents create comprehensive, engaging summaries.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Multi-Format Output">
    Get research summaries, vector embeddings for Q&A (Qdrant + OpenAI), auto-generated FAQs, visual mindmaps (react-mindmap-visualiser), and podcast-style audio overviews (LemonFox TTS).
  </Step>
</Steps>
